Capabilities
& Craft

Woodworking

Crafted with precision and artistry, our woodworking transforms raw materials into custom pieces that define spaces. From intricate millwork to statement furniture, we create custom wood designs that elevate interiors with warmth and craftsmanship.

Hardware

All in the details, hardware is what ties a product together, offering both utility and visual impact. From expertly crafted handles to bespoke hinges and fittings, each piece is engineered for precision, durability, and seamless integration into any design.

Leatherwork

Luxurious leatherwork that speaks to both sophistication and resilience, tailored to elevate every design. Using graded-in or COL (customer's own leather), our pieces undergo rigorous strength testing to ensure they meet the highest hospitality standards.

CNC Machining

Precision and creativity converge in our CNC machining, where cutting-edge technology produces flawless, intricate components. We specialize in transforming designs into tangible elements that enhance bespoke interiors and furniture.

Metal Plating

Custom metal plating that elevates the beauty and resilience of furniture and fixtures in high-performance environments. Sleek, polished, and refined, our custom solutions enhance furniture, hardware, and architectural details with long-lasting brilliance.

Powder Coating

Sleek and durable powder coating designed to protect and enhance black steel surfaces. Engineered for longevity, our coatings deliver exceptional resilience for furniture, fixtures, and architectural details in high-demand spaces.

Stone

Timeless stonework crafted with precision to enhance interiors with sophistication and strength. From countertops to decorative inlays, our designs are engineered to resist wear and deliver enduring beauty.

Upholstery

Upholstery that redefines luxury, balancing comfort, elegance, and resilience for high-end interiors. Every piece is strength-tested and crafted with materials engineered to meet the rigorous demands of hospitality, ensuring unmatched quality and style.

Finishing

Finishes that preserve and enhance the integrity of every material, applied with meticulous care. From protecting natural beauty to ensuring resilience, our techniques add a refined touch to hospitality and residential pieces.

Realization &
Implementation

Prototyping

Prototyping brings your ideas to life, bridging the gap between imagination and execution. Every detail is explored and refined, ensuring the final product feels just as good as it looks.

Case Goods

Thoughtfully designed case goods that offer practicality without compromising on style. From sleek cabinetry to custom storage solutions, each piece is built to fit seamlessly into its environment and enhance its purpose.

Soft Goods

Soft goods that do more than add comfort—they bring personality and warmth to a space. From tailored cushions to elegant drapery, we create pieces that balance texture and design for a polished finish.

Millwork

Millwork that turns architectural features into defining moments of a space. Whether it’s custom paneling, intricate moldings, or built-in furniture, our millwork is designed to elevate the story of your interiors.

Outdoor Furniture

Outdoor furniture that feels as effortless as it is enduring. Designed to weather the elements with style, our pieces create inviting outdoor settings for gathering, dining, or simply unwinding.

Banquettes

Banquettes that make a statement while inviting people to stay a little longer. Tailored to your space, they combine thoughtful proportions, refined finishes, and plush comfort to create the perfect seating solution.

Joinery

Joinery that reflects precision, expertise, and timeless craftsmanship. Every connection is carefully considered, ensuring strength and elegance that anchors furniture and spaces for years to come.

Scopes
& Spaces

Hotels

Hotels are where first impressions meet lasting memories. From grand public spaces to intimate guest rooms, we craft solutions that blend functionality, comfort, and elegance to define the guest experience.

Guest Rooms

Guest rooms should feel like a retreat, balancing personal comfort with thoughtful design. Our custom furnishings and finishes create spaces that are as welcoming as they are refined.

Public Spaces

Public spaces are the heartbeat of a property, designed to bring people together. We create furniture and details that inspire connection, ensuring every moment spent here feels intentional.

Lobbies

Lobbies set the tone, offering a first impression that lingers. Through bespoke furniture and millwork, we shape environments that are equal parts inviting and impressive.

Reception Areas

Reception areas are more than transitional spaces—they’re moments of arrival. With carefully designed desks and seating, we ensure every interaction feels polished and professional.

Lounges

Lounges are where sophistication meets relaxation. From plush seating to curated furnishings, we create settings that encourage guests to linger and unwind.

Food and Beverage

Food and beverage spaces deserve designs as memorable as the menus they serve. We craft seating, banquettes, and millwork that elevate dining experiences while reflecting each unique concept.

Outdoor Terrace

Outdoor terraces are where the line between nature and design blurs. Our furniture and finishes are built to withstand the elements while offering a warm, stylish invitation to enjoy the outdoors.

Office

Offices should reflect professionalism without sacrificing comfort. We design furniture that supports productivity and creates spaces where people want to work.

Residences

Residences are a reflection of personal style and comfort. From bespoke furniture to tailored finishes, we help transform living spaces into sanctuaries that feel uniquely yours.
